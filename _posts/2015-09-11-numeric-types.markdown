## Numbers in Java

 type   | bits   | min        | max
------- | ------ | ---------- | -----------
 byte   | 8      | $-128$     | $127$
 short  | 16     | $-2^{15}$  | $2^{15}-1$
 int    | 32     | $-2^{31}$  | $2^{31}-1$
 long   | 64     | $-2^{63}$  | $2^{63}-1$
